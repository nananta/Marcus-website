About Me

Hi, I'm Marcus Ananta, a dedicated high school student from Santa Susana High School with a passion for law and justice. My journey toward becoming a law professional started when I developed an interest in how legal systems shape our society.

Through my academic pursuits and social media platforms, I share my experiences navigating high school while preparing for a future legal career. I'm committed to building a strong foundation for law school through debate club participation, mock trial competitions, and community service.

I also offer tutoring services in English, History, Debate, and Pre-Law topics to help other students succeed. My teaching approach focuses on building confidence and critical thinking skills.

Recent Achievement: Proud to be recognized by Rotary as Santa Susana High School's Student of the Year for my academic excellence and community involvement.
